Welcome!

My name is Ituze Bonheur, and I’m a 16-year-old male student at Mather High School. I was born on October 7th, 2009, in Zambia, a country in Southern Africa. In 2024, I moved to the United States, where I now live and continue my studies.

I enjoy exploring technology — I like learning how Android, Microsoft, Google, and Samsung products work, and I often try customizing my devices.

What Does Ituze Bonheur Mean?

My first name, Ituze, comes from the Kinyarwanda language and means peace or calmness. My middle name, Bonheur, is a French word that means happiness or good fortune. Together, my full name stands for peace and happiness — values that guide who I am and how I live my life.

Although I have three names, I usually share only these two. They speak for me in a simple, clear way while capturing the qualities I value most: calm, happiness, and purpose. These names are not just identifiers; they are a reflection of who I am and how I wish to interact with the world.
